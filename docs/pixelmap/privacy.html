Pixelmap — Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 3, 2026

Pixelmap ("the app") is an exploration map app that reveals a pixel-art map of the areas you have physically visited. This policy explains what data the app uses and how it is handled.

The short version

Pixelmap does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device. The app has no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind.

Location data

  • The app uses your device's location only while the app is in use and only after you explicitly start exploration.
  • Location data is used to reveal explored areas on the map and is stored solely in a local database on your device.
  • Location data is never transmitted to the developer or to any third-party server.
  • You can stop location use at any time with the pause button, or revoke the permission in iOS Settings.

Map tiles (OpenFreeMap)

To draw the map, the app downloads map tile data over HTTPS from OpenFreeMap, a free map hosting service based on OpenStreetMap data. As with any web request, the tile server technically receives your IP address and the coordinates of the map tiles being displayed. These requests contain no user identifiers, no cookies, and no account information, and the app sends them only to render the map. OpenFreeMap states that it does not log personally identifiable information; see the OpenFreeMap website for details.

Data storage and deletion

All exploration records (visited map cells and explored points) are stored in a local SQLite database inside the app's private sandbox on your device. They are included in your device backups if you use iCloud/computer backups. Deleting the app permanently deletes all of this data. There is nothing to delete on any server, because no server stores your data.

Third-party services

The app contains no advertising SDKs, no analytics SDKs, and no social media integrations. The only network communication is the map tile download described above.

Children's privacy

The app is not directed at children and collects no personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If the app's data practices ever change, this policy will be updated and the effective date above will be revised before the change takes effect.
